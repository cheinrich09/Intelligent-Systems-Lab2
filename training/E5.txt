Thomas de Zengotita (born c. 1944) is an author and contributing editor at Harper's Magazine. He holds a Ph.D. in anthropology from Columbia University and teaches at the Dalton School and New York University. His most recent book is called Mediated: How the Media Shapes Your World and the Way You Live in It (2005). In 2010, he narrated a film directed by Adrian Grenier entitled Teenage Paparazzo. De Zengotita is also a contributor to the Huffington Post blog.
